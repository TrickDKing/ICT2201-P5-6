Quick start guide to get you started!

How do i play this game?

    The rule of this game is simple, make your way to the endpoint and earn as much point as possible!

    Drag and drop blocks to control how your car moves!

    Pick up one of these to gain extra points!

    Watch out for obstacles along the way! You cant proceed further upon hitting one!

    Watch out for debuff! You health will drop when you pick up one

    Your game ends when you run out of health or when you reach the end! Check the high score board to see your best attempts!
